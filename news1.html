NEWSROOM


Central College is pleased to announce that Isabelle Moncion has been named its new Director of Communications and Corporate Affairs. Isabelle Moncion is a fluently bilingual communications manager and professional with over ten years of experience in communications and education. Isabelle brings with her a modern vision of communications and a variety of experiences in various communications specialities that should serve the college well. Prior to coming to Central College, Isabelle served as the Head of Communications for Cégep de Saint-Laurent leading a team responsible for internal and external communications. Additionally, she has worked at the Service régional d’admission du Montréal métropolitain (SRAM), Université de Montréal and in the private sector. Isabelle Moncion holds a Masters in Political Science from UQAM, a Bachelor of Social Science in Communications and Political Science from the University of Ottawa and a DESS in Management Communications-Marketing from HEC, Montréal. “I’m honoured to be joining Central College, well-known for its dedication to building a more sustainable and equitable future for our communities. I look forward to using my knowledge and experience in education and in communications to support Central’s mission and further strengthening the institution’s unique positioning in higher education” said Isabelle Moncion. Isabelle will commence her new role at Central College on May 2, 2022.
